Welcome to my crypto tools that work on data from Bittrex

I created some tooling because the charts on Bittrex are almost 5 minutes old. So its hard to see what happens in the past 5 minutes with the actual price without remembering all lots of small numbers.

Currently there are 3 tools:

  • Global overview sorted on price
  • Price of a coin per second
  • 24 hour data with avarages of last 1 and 2 hours

For covering some of the costs https://coin-hive.com/ is used with a single thread to mine some XMR using your browser.

If you contribute or donate to keep the site running you will get a mine free version
